The Chicken Coop

At "Jake’s Chicken Shack", we firmly believe in customer loyalty. We want our customers to leave happy satisfied, and eager to come back. To reach this goal, we have created our own unique reward program, The Chicken Coop.

Members of The Chicken Coop will receive a membership card and earn a point after every purchase of a meal. Members who buy 10 meals will get the 11th on us, absolutely free! Additionally, members will receive a free dessert of their choice on their birthday*. Interested? Join below, it’s free!

Newsletter

When you sign up for The Chicken Coop, you can also opt in for our newsletter! Keep up to date on all of our new items and promotions. As a member of The Chicken Coop, you are also eligible to opt in for our monthly raffle. Our Chicken Coop raffle allows our members a chance to win cool prizes like free food, drinks and other restaurant benefits.

* With the purchase of a drink, meal, or side.
      Not valid with the purchase of a dessert.
