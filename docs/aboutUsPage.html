About Us

"Jake’s Chicken Shack" is the premier "high end" fast food chicken joint in the country. "Jake’s" is known for their flavorful chicken and whopping portions, as well as their hospitality and top-notch customer service.

Established in 2009, "Jake’s" was founded with a goal - to serve the best food around with a smile. The first location was opened in West Rogers Park, Jake’s childhood neighborhood. The staff quickly gained the favorable opinions of the neighborhood, and after a short period, they decided to expand the company, opening their second location in Lincoln Park in 2010.

Meet the Team


The expansion proved to be a fruitful move, as word of mouth and the expertise of Jeff Makoroff (known for many successful fast food marketing campaigns) cemented "Jake’s" as the first choice when looking for wholesome soul food.

The staff decided to strike while the iron was hot, opening 3 more locations in Wilmette, Niles and Skokie; all locations adopting and upholding the goal that the original location strived for - best food around with a smile.

Since, the company has thrived. We at "Jake’s" want to be able to spread our food and life mantras to everyone, and we hope that you enjoy or food!

- Jake Espana and the Chicken Shack Team

Christian Racho, Head Chef

Christian, the head chef and culinary expert, was brought on board in the summer of 2010. After Jake decided that it would be too hard to manage AND cook, he was referred to Christian by a mutual friend.

Christian was trained classically at the Cordon Bleu Culinary School, but parted from his roots by accepting the position of Head Chef at "Jake’s". Now dealing primarily in the soul food that "Jake’s" is well known for, Christian originally trained to work in the French culinary world.

Jeff Makoroff, Head of Marketing

Realizing that he would need proper marketing in order for the business to grow, Jake enlisted the help of his longtime friend Jeff.

With an extensive background in fast food marketing - having helped launch the "I’m Lovin It" McDonalds ad campaign as well as the "America Runs on Dunkin’" ad campaign for Dunkin’ Donuts - Jeff was the perfect man to help grow the "Jake’s" brand name.

Coming aboard during the Summer of 2011, Jeff has found a permanent home at "Jake’s Chicken Shack" and it looks like he is here to stay.

Joseph Koss, Head of Operations

With a need for organization after the quick and explosive expansion of “Jake’s”, Jake decided to search for a top notch Operations Manager. He found one in 2011 with Joseph Koss, former COO for Popeye’s and Long John Silvers.

Joe quickly whipped the company in shape, implementing different operational strategies to streamline the operations of the many locations. With a knack for logistics and efficiency, Joe has established “Jake’s” as one of the more organized and clean “high end” fast food restaurants in the country.

Jacob Espana, Owner and Founder

Jacob, the namesake of the franchise, started off as a kitchen hand in high school. Slowly observing and absorbing his surroundings allowed him to learn the craft and grow his love for the art of cooking.

Eventually, Jacob decided to venture out and open his own chicken restaurant in 2009, the first "Jake’s Chicken Shack".

Nowadays, Jake likes to spend his off days doing amateur web design and experimenting with different recipes.
